Features

CustomBoot allows you to change the boot logo (NOT THE RESPRING LOGO). After installation you can choose a new boot logo in settings.

Known issues

  • The package should make a backup of your boot logo in "/Library/Application Support/CustomBoot", if it didn't make this backup or your boot logo is blank after removal of the pacage you should contact @Flipp3rrr on Twitter or flipp3rrr@protonmail.com via email.
